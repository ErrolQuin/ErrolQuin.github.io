About this page

This webpage serves as a showcase for my work in the Programming Languages course at Oswego. Included are various materials such as programming assignments, code, and demos. Additionally, you will find work on problem sets, including language description exercises and written pieces pertaining to programming languages. The content is organized in chronological order based on their respective due dates, and each item is clearly identified by means of metatext and hypertext.
